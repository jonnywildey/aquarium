Aquarium

An immersive sonic exploration crafted for aquarium enthusiasts and inhabitants. Compositions designed to complement the serene environments of aquatic spaces, drawing inspiration from the depths of oceans, the flow of rivers, and the quiet mysteries of underwater worlds.

Each track has been carefully composed to cultivate observation and integration with the environment, creating an atmosphere of calm reflection and wonder that resonates with the endless rhythm of water.
