Aquarium

An immersive sonic exploration crafted for aquarium professionals and enthusiasts. Ten compositions designed to complement the serene environments of aquatic spaces, drawing inspiration from the depths of oceans, the flow of rivers, and the quiet mysteries of underwater worlds.

Each track has been carefully composed to enhance the contemplative experience of observing aquatic life, creating an atmosphere of calm reflection and wonder that resonates with the timeless rhythm of water.
